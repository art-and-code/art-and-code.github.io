art & code: motion, pattern & shape

5:00pm at wepress every 2nd + last sunday

Held at wepress, a community arts space located on unceded Squamish, Musqueam, and Tsleil-Waututh land.

We will use creative code to make art! in a comfy, no-bro space. Delicious snacks and working laptops will be provided.

All workshops are free, and open to women-identified, genderqueer, non-binary, queer & poc folks who self-identify as experiencing mental health or addictions issues.

no experience with programming (or art) is required!
